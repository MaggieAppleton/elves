# Elves Logo Exploration

## Purpose

Create six simple logo concepts for Elves, a local-first writing studio where agents help organize and critique a writer's work without writing the prose. The identity should suggest quiet editorial craft and helpful intelligence, not fantasy illustration.

## Deliverable

One comparison sheet containing six distinct logo concepts. Each concept includes a compact symbol and an `Elves` wordmark lockup. The sheet is exploratory artwork, intended to identify a direction before a final SVG is drawn.

## Visual Constraints

- Use only black, white, and at most one neutral grey.
- Use flat, hard-edged geometry with no gradients, shadows, textures, or mockups.
- Keep every symbol legible at app-icon size and suitable for later SVG reconstruction.
- Avoid mascots, faces, detailed fantasy imagery, sparkles, and generic AI symbolism.
- Use one restrained wordmark treatment across all six concepts.

## Concepts

1. **Nib Ear**: a pen nib containing an elf-ear counterform.
2. **Quote Ear**: a pointed ear constructed from quotation-mark geometry.
3. **Page Cap**: a folded page corner that also reads as an elf cap.
4. **Written E**: an `E` constructed from three manuscript lines.
5. **Nib E**: a compact `E` incorporating a pen-nib counterform.
6. **Page E**: an `E` cut from a simple page silhouette using negative space.

## Evaluation Criteria

- Immediately recognizable silhouette
- Clear relationship to writing, elves, or both
- Legible in symbol-only and symbol-plus-wordmark contexts
- Distinct from generic writing-app and AI-assistant marks
- Practical to recreate as clean SVG paths
